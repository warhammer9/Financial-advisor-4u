What's a good strategy for a beginner investor with ₹1,00,000?
F
Great starting point! Here's a beginner-friendly strategy for ₹1,00,000 in India:

1. Emergency Fund First — Keep 3–6 months of expenses in a high-interest savings account or liquid mutual fund before investing.

2. Index Mutual Funds via SIP — Allocate ~70% to Nifty 50 or Nifty Next 50 index funds. Low expense ratios, great diversification.

3. Debt Fund Exposure — ~20% in short-duration debt funds for stability.

4. Growth Allocation — ~10% in mid-cap or sectoral funds if you have higher risk tolerance.

Is now a good time to invest in crypto?
F
Crypto can play a role in a diversified portfolio, but here's what to keep in mind — especially in India:

⚠️ High volatility — Bitcoin has seen 50–80% drawdowns historically. Only invest what you can afford to lose.

🇮🇳 Indian Tax Rules — Crypto gains are taxed at a flat 30% in India, plus 1% TDS on every transaction. This significantly impacts returns.

✅ Limit exposure — Most advisors recommend 5–10% max of your portfolio in crypto.

📌 SIP into crypto — Instead of a lump sum, invest a fixed amount monthly via platforms like CoinDCX or WazirX to reduce timing risk.

The "best time" to invest is when you have a long-term horizon (3–5+ years) and a solid financial foundation.
